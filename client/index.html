Tuas and Cecil is a Singapore based rough diamond trading company.
We source our rough diamonds all over the world from conflict free sources. Our people roam the forests and winter tundra in order to find the rarest clear and coloured diamonds available in the earths crust.
Our story started when our founder asked the same question most of our customers ask. Where do diamonds come from?
Exploring that curiosity has lead us to become one of the few rough diamond trading companies that fully understands and follows our diamonds from source to ring.
Tuas and Cecil strictly follows the internationally recognised Kimberly Process.
