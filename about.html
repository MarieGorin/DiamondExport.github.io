IN THE JUNGLE SEARCHING FOR DIAMONDS
Because we believe our customers deserve the purest diamonds,
we select and pick them ourselves,
follow closely their shipment,
and witness their cut and refinement firsthand.

THE DIAMOND JOURNEY

First step

We meet with diamond exporters at the source,
in conflict free African countries,
to observe, evaluate and select, one by one,
each diamond from the batch.


Second step

We retrieve our agreed batch
directly from our contact
and transit them through safe urban areas
to receive the approved and legit certificates.


Third step

We export our diamonds from Africa
and securely ship them by plane to Hong Kong,
where we double-check their quality and purity.


Fourth step

We move our diamonds to India
where they are within the hands of expert jewelers,
adnd individually refined, polished and cleaned
to take the famous yet various diamond shapes.


Fifth step

Our diamonds are shiped to Singapore,
where we ensure their quality and purity one last time
while gathering the appropriate trading documents.


Fifth step
Finally, the batch is divided
and sub-batched are securely sent to be sold,
piece by piece and directly from us,
in Singapore, Hong Kong, and the USA.
